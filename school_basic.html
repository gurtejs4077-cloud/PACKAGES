How to Apply

Applications for the 2025–26 academic year are now open. Our admissions process is transparent, merit-based, and designed to identify potential — not just performance.

01

Submit Inquiry Form

Fill our online form or visit the admissions office. Receive a registration kit within 48 hours.

02

Campus Visit & Interaction

Schedule a school tour. Students and parents meet with our academic counsellors.

03

Assessment & Interview

Grade-appropriate assessment. Friendly, informal — designed to reveal strengths, not rank students.

04

Offer & Enrolment

Receive your offer letter and complete enrolment with our dedicated admissions coordinator.
